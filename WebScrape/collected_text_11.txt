Sabrina Carpenter has announced a North American tour this year in support of her upcoming album, Short N' Sweet.

The 29-date Short N' Sweet Tour across cities in the U.S. and Canada will begin in Columbus, Ohio, on September 23, and will conclude in Los Angeles on November 15.

Amaarae, Griff and Declan McKenna will support Sabrina during the tour.

Proceeds from the concerts will be used to raise funds to launch the Sabrina Carpenter Fund, in partnership with PLUS1.

The fund "will support the well-being of people and animals, including national and local organizations working on the issues of mental health, animal welfare, and support for the LGBTQ+ community," according to a press release.

Presale of tickets will begin on Monday, June 24, with the Cash App Presale at 10 a.m. local time followed by Team Sabrina presale on Tuesday, June 25, at 10 a.m. local time. The general on-sale will start Friday, June 28, at 10 a.m. local time.

The 25 year-old singer and actress will release her sixth full-length studio album, Short n' Sweet, on August 23.
